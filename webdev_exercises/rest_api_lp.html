About Us

We are a group of fans really passionate about Star Wars. After hours of watching films we came to the conclusion that there must be a fast and easy way to learn more about all the characters. On 2018 we finally decided to build this online encyclopedia using the Star Wars API which is the world's first quantified and programmatically-formatted set of Star Wars data. Our future plans include not only People but Films, Species, Starships, Vehicles and Planets from Star Wars.
